**Privacy Policy for Planeshot**

*Effective Date: 09/28/2025*

**Introduction**

Welcome to Planeshot, a game developed by ForwardSlashGames. We value your privacy and want to assure you that your personal information is safe while you enjoy our game. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use Planeshot.

**Information We Collect**

At ForwardSlashGames, we want to emphasize that Planeshot does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not gather or store any information from your device or your usage of the game. Our commitment to privacy means that you can enjoy Planeshot without concerns about data collection or monitoring.

**Use of Information**

Since we do not collect any information from users, we do not use personal data for any purpose, including:

- Improving our game
- Marketing
- User analytics

**Disclosure of Information**

As we do not gather any information from our users, there is no information to disclose to third parties. ForwardSlashGames does not share, sell, or trade any user data.

**Third-Party Services**

Planeshot does not integrate or utilize any third-party services that would collect or track your personal information. While you may have the option to interact with other players through game features, we do not monitor or store such interactions.

**Children’s Privacy**

Planeshot is intended for users of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise their children’s online activities.

**Security**

Even though we do not collect personal information, we still take reasonable measures to protect our game from unauthorized access and to ensure that we provide a safe gaming experience.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

ForwardSlashGames reserves the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users by providing notice through the game or updating the effective date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
